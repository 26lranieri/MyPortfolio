Design Portfolio Guidelines

A design portfolio is a visual collection of an engineer's work and ideas in cronological order that went into the design of a product.
Its purpose is to protect your work by proving the orgin of an idea, diligence in turning an idea into a product, and provide evidence of when and idea became a working product.
